I am presently an undergraduate student (junior) at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of Southern University of Science and Technology. I am enthusiastic about deep learning, with a specific focus on large language models (LLMs) and automated program repair (APR). I am also interested in language learning and can program in C, C++, Java, Python, HTML+JavaScript+CSS and speak Spanish and Russian as third and fourth languages respectively.
